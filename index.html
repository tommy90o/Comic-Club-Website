Comic Club

About us

On 2 July 2024, when everyone had gone on a field trip, the founder of the Comic Club stayed back, saying there would be “too much traffic.” So at home, he and his friend (the co-founder) made a comic and came up with one of the most brilliant ideas ever: to start a club. Being the person he is, he called it the Comic Club. As more of his friends joined, the Comic Club expanded and now has a variety of comics from many creators.

Types of comics

There are 3 types of comics:
1. Funny comics-
We guarenee that you will laugh more than 3 times reading one of them(Even if it's because it wasn't funny, just plain silly!)
2. Adventure comics-
We guarantee at least one daring escape, two impossible missions, and a hero who somehow survives everything!
3. Gaming comics-
We guarantee at least one rage quit, three “last tries,” and a victory that was totally skill.
